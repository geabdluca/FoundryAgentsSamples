# Contoso Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Contoso

Contoso is a global technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington, with offices in more than 40 countries. Our mission is to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more through innovative technology solutions.

## Code of Conduct

All Contoso employees are expected to act with integrity, treat colleagues and customers with respect, and uphold the highest ethical standards. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or retaliation is grounds for immediate disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

## Work Hours and Flexibility

Contoso operates on a flexible hybrid model. Most roles require a minimum of two days per week in the office; the remaining days may be worked remotely. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time Monday through Friday. Teams may agree on additional overlap hours as needed.

## Time Off

Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year, increasing to 20 days after three years of service and 25 days after seven years. Contoso also observes 11 company holidays annually. Additionally, employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave, which does not roll over at year end.

## Benefits

Contoso offers comprehensive benefits including medical, dental, and vision insurance, a 401(k) plan with a 4% company match, employee stock purchase plan (ESPP), tuition reimbursement up to $5,250 per year, and a $1,200 annual wellness stipend. Full benefits details are available on the Contoso HR portal.

## Performance Reviews

Performance is evaluated twice a year: mid-year check-ins in June and annual reviews in December. Employees are assessed on goal achievement, competencies, and overall impact. Compensation adjustments and bonus eligibility are tied to the annual review cycle.

## IT and Security Policy

Employees must use Contoso-issued devices or approved personal devices enrolled in mobile device management (MDM) for any work-related activity. Sharing credentials or bypassing multi-factor authentication (MFA) is strictly prohibited. All data classified as Confidential or above must be stored in approved Contoso systems only.

# Contoso Travel and Expense Policy

## Overview

This policy applies to all Contoso full-time employees, contractors, and interns who incur business-related travel or entertainment expenses. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Late submissions may be denied.

## Booking Travel

All business travel must be booked through the Contoso Travel Portal (travel.contoso.com) or through our preferred travel agency, Global Business Travel (GBT). Booking outside these channels requires pre-approval from your manager and Finance, and reimbursement is capped at the rates available through the portal.

## Airfare

Economy class is required for flights under 6 hours. Business class may be approved for flights over 6 hours with VP-level approval. Employees should book at least 14 days in advance to minimize cost. Upgrades purchased personally are not reimbursable.

## Hotels

The nightly hotel rate limit by city:

| City | Limit (USD, per night) |
|---|---|
| Redmond / Seattle | $220 |
| New York City | $350 |
| San Francisco / San Jose | $300 |
| London | £250 |
| Other US cities | $180 |
| Other international | $200 |

Employees are expected to use Contoso's preferred hotel partners where available, as negotiated rates typically fall within policy limits.

## Meals and Per Diem

Daily meal limits (excluding alcohol):

- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $60

Alcohol is never reimbursable as a standalone expense. When entertaining clients, alcohol may be included in the meal total with a note in the expense report; client entertainment meals have a combined limit of $150 per person.

## Ground Transportation

Use ride-share services (Uber, Lyft) or public transportation where practical. Rental cars require manager approval and must be booked through the travel portal. Personal vehicle use is reimbursed at the current IRS standard mileage rate.

## Expense Reporting

Expense reports are submitted via the Contoso Finance Portal (finance.contoso.com). Receipts are required for any single expense over $25. Expenses are reimbursed to your payroll account within 10 business days of approval.

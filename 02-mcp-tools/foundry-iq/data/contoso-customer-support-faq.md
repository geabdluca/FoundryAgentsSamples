# Contoso Customer Support FAQ

## General Questions

**Q: How do I contact Contoso customer support?**
A: You can reach Contoso Support via:
- Online portal: support.contoso.com (24/7 ticket submission and live chat for Premium customers)
- Phone: 1-800-CONTOSO (Monday–Friday 6:00 AM–6:00 PM Pacific Time)
- Email: support@contoso.com (response within 2 business days)

**Q: What are your support tiers?**
A: Contoso offers three support plans:
- **Basic** (included with all products): Access to the self-service knowledge base and community forums. Email support with 2 business day response SLA.
- **Professional**: 8×5 phone and chat support, 4-hour response for critical issues, named support engineer.
- **Premium**: 24×7 phone and chat support, 1-hour response for P1 incidents, dedicated technical account manager (TAM), quarterly business reviews.

## Billing and Licensing

**Q: How do I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?**
A: Log in to your Contoso account at account.contoso.com, navigate to **Subscriptions**, and select **Change Plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.

**Q: Can I add more licenses mid-term?**
A: Yes. Additional licenses are prorated to your current renewal date. You can add seats from the **Subscriptions** page or by contacting your Contoso account manager.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Contoso accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, ACH bank transfer, and purchase orders (for annual contracts over $10,000). Invoices are available for annual plans.

## Technical Issues

**Q: My Contoso CloudSuite login is not working. What should I do?**
A: First, try resetting your password at account.contoso.com/reset. If you are still unable to log in, check your company's identity provider (SSO) status page. If the issue persists, contact your company's Contoso administrator or open a support ticket.

**Q: How do I report a security vulnerability?**
A: Contoso takes security seriously. Please report vulnerabilities responsibly via our security disclosure portal at security.contoso.com/report. Do not submit vulnerability details through the standard support portal. Our security team will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours and keep you updated throughout the investigation.

**Q: Where can I find release notes and changelogs?**
A: Release notes for all Contoso products are published at docs.contoso.com/release-notes. You can subscribe to email notifications for specific products from your account settings.

## Data and Privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: By default, Contoso stores customer data in the United States. Customers on the Enterprise plan can choose data residency in the EU (Germany or Ireland), UK, or Australia. Data residency preferences are configured during onboarding and can be changed with 30 days' notice.

**Q: How do I request a copy of my data?**
A: Submit a Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) via the Contoso Privacy Portal at privacy.contoso.com. Requests are fulfilled within 30 days in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

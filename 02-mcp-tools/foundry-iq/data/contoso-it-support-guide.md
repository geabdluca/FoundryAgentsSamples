# Contoso IT Support Guide

## How to Get Help

Contoso employees can reach the IT Help Desk through the following channels:

- **Self-service portal**: helpdesk.contoso.com — log tickets, track status, access the knowledge base
- **Chat**: Available in the Contoso intranet Monday–Friday 7:00 AM–8:00 PM local time
- **Phone**: 1-800-CONTOSO (1-800-266-8676), 24/7 for Priority 1 (system-down) incidents
- **Walk-in**: IT service desks are located on floors 2 and 5 at Redmond HQ and at all major regional offices

## Password Reset

To reset your Contoso account password without IT assistance, visit account.contoso.com and click "Forgot password". You will be prompted to verify your identity via the Microsoft Authenticator app or an SMS code sent to your registered phone number.

If you are locked out and cannot access self-service, call the Help Desk at 1-800-CONTOSO and complete verbal identity verification.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

All Contoso accounts require MFA. The supported methods are Microsoft Authenticator app (preferred), hardware security key (FIDO2), and SMS one-time passcode (fallback only — not recommended). If you need to register a new MFA device, submit a ticket via helpdesk.contoso.com with your manager's approval.

## Requesting Software

Software requests must be submitted through the IT portal under "Software & Licenses". Requests require manager approval. Standard catalog software (listed at helpdesk.contoso.com/catalog) is provisioned within one business day. Non-catalog software requires a security review, which takes up to five business days.

## Laptop Replacement and Repairs

Laptops that are under warranty are repaired or replaced within three business days. Accidental damage claims must be filed within 48 hours of the incident. Contact the Help Desk and complete the Incident Report form. Loaner devices are available at the Redmond HQ service desk while your device is being serviced.

## VPN and Remote Access

Contoso uses Always-On VPN for corporate network access. The VPN client is pre-installed on all Contoso-managed devices and connects automatically when off the corporate network. If you experience connectivity issues, restart the VPN client via the system tray icon or contact the Help Desk.

## Data Classification

| Level | Examples | Handling |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Press releases, marketing materials | No restrictions |
| Internal | Internal wikis, org charts | Do not share externally |
| Confidential | Customer data, financial reports | Encrypt in transit and at rest; approved systems only |
| Restricted | HR records, M&A data | Need-to-know access; legal approval required |
